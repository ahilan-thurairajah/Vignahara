Meet Your Ideal Tenants

Understanding your audience is key. Your marketing should speak to two distinct groups, each with their own motivations and needs. Remember, you're often speaking to their tech-savvy adult children as well, so professionalism is paramount.

Segment A: The Active Pre/Post-Retiree (50-65)

Digitally proficient and active, this group is downsizing for lifestyle freedom. They're looking for convenience and community, not care.

  • **Motivations:** Reduce home maintenance, unlock equity, pursue hobbies.
  • **Values:** Independence, convenience, recreation, social opportunities.
  • **Where to Reach Them:** Realtor.ca, Zumper, Facebook Marketplace.
  • **Key Message:** "Enjoy a lock-and-leave lifestyle with everything you need at your doorstep."

Segment B: The Established Retiree (65-75)

While still able, this group places a higher premium on security and proactive healthcare access. Trust is their primary currency.

  • **Motivations:** Future-proofing their living situation, simplicity, safety.
  • **Values:** Security, comfort, proximity to healthcare, community belonging.
  • **Where to Reach Them:** 55+ Centres, RVH referrals, community word-of-mouth.
  • **Key Message:** "Live with peace of mind in a safe, quiet home, just moments from world-class healthcare."
